The Discovery

"After the pain left, I was scared! I could actually 'see' inside things and knew things I shouldn't!"

"Unknowingly I had become a Medical Intuitive."

What happened to me is typical for a Medical Intuitive. Forty plus years ago I stopped in the left hand turn lane for a red light. I glanced up just as the car behind struck my pickup. The police said the teen was going approximately 50 m.p.h. at impact. He told them that he thought he could beat the light, that he just didn't see me.

My head flew forward into the windshield then snapped back into the rear window. The truck went up, came down, was struck again, causing my head to slam into the side window.

After the pain eased, I was really scared! I could actually see inside things. I knew things a normal person couldn't know. I found that I could get rid of the pain people were in. I'd had no exposure to the metaphysical, new age world or quantum physics. I didn't understand what was going on.

During my convalescence, I stayed with my parents so that my chiropractor father could treat me. As it happened, while sitting in his waiting room waiting for treatment, I realized that I knew what had happened to the woman sitting next to me and what she needed to have done to recover.

Scared, I told my father what I heard. He wasn't scared. He was excited. He told me that her x-rays verified what I'd heard.

He said that he'd read something about what I was describing. (This all took place long before home computers and the internet.) He'd read about men with abilities similar to what I was experiencing.

Edgar Cayce a psychic healer, Peter Hurkos a carpenter who turned psychic when he hit his head falling from a ladder, and Dr. Phineas Parkhurst Quimby who in the mid-1800's told his patients that he knew how to heal as Jesus did. It's recorded that he healed over 38,000 people.

Before then I don't think I'd ever heard the word psychic. As I found out more about these men, I compared their experiences to mine, I realized that I wasn't crazy or possessed. I was actually "seeing" inside bodies and "hearing" things.

As a Medical Intuitive my abilities go way beyond seeing what's wrong... I know what to do to fix what I see.

An Actual Demonstration of Quimby's Method

One hundred twenty-five doctors were given an opportunity to observe the dramatic results of Quimby's Method.

As the result of a shower glass door accident, Lisa Franklin spent the evening in an a hospital emergency room. Her hand needed She required 8 stitches. Lisa's husband was one of the professionals doctors scheduled to attend my demonstration the next day. He called to ask if his wife could be one of my demonstrations. She was in horrible pain. I told him that as long as she can remain drug free for the demonstration, I'd love to use her.

The following morning Lisa arrived pale from pain. Holding her hand up to avoid an increase of blood flow and even more pain, she was cupping her elbow. I asked her to come forward and sit on the tall bar stool placed at the front of the room.

Five of the doctors examined her hand. It was a puffy, red, swollen mass with four, thick black X's where the emergency room doctor had put stitches. Their conclusion: Her hand was typical of one that had suffered a deep cut within the previous 18 hours.

I stood beside her so the audience could watch. Thirty minutes into the session, Lisa didn't seem to notice (but the audience did) that she'd put her hand down when she laughed. An hour later, still not touching her, I asked if her hand hurt.

The doctors re-examined her hand. There was no sign of an injury; just four black X's. The skin showed no sign that it had been pulled back together. No puffiness. No redness. No thin red line.
