An Actual Demonstration of Quimby's Method

One hundred twenty-five doctors were given an opportunity to observe the dramatic results of my Sessions using Quimby's Method.

As the result of a shower glass door accident, Lisa Franklin spent the evening in a hospital emergency room. Her hand needed eight stitches. Lisa's husband was one of the doctors scheduled to attend my demonstration the next morning. He called to me to ask if his wife could be used in the morning for one of my demonstrations. She was in horrible pain.

"Of course," I said, "I'd love to do it provided she can remain drug free for the demonstration."

Lisa arrived, the following morning, pale from the pain. She was cupping her elbow, holding her hand up to avoid an increase of blood flow and more severe pain. I asked her come forward and sit on the tall bar stool I had placed at the front of the room.

Five of the doctors examined her hand. It was a puffy, red, swollen mass with four, thick black X's where the emergency room doctor had put the stitches. Their conclusion, her hand was typical of one that had suffered a deep cut within the previous 18 hours.

I stood beside her so the audience could watch. Thirty minutes into the session, Lisa didn't seem to notice (but the audience did) that she'd put her hand down when she laughed. An hour later, still not touching her, I asked if her hand hurt.

She looked bewildered, as if she'd forgotten why she was sitting in front of 125 doctors. At first she gently felt the area, then she poked it. What she did next surprised even me. She slammed it against the edge of the stool several times. She looked a little confused when she said, "There's no pain. It doesn't hurt at all."

The doctors re-examined her hand. There was no sign of an injury; just four black X's. The skin showed no sign that it had been pulled back together. No puffiness. No redness. No thin red line.

Dr. Russell Loveland, a Chiropractor in practice for over twenty years, was in the audience.

"The first time that I heard there was someone who knew how to heal using logic alone, I knew they had something. After that demonstration, I realized that this method is the Lamborghini of Healing."
